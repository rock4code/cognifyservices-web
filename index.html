An Astro Starter batteries-included

A fully responsive astro starter repo, ideal for simple (yet laborious) content-driven pages or landings.

Get the Code

Tired of writing the same code over and over again?

We've all been there... Setting up the same SEO, theming, and responsive layout boilerplate again and again. Say goodbye to that! alfadev-astro-starter lets you jump straight into creating, skipping the repetitive groundwork.

This is not a library

It's a ready-to-go template. No steep learning curves or dozens of new dependencies. Just the essentials to build content-driven pages with ease.
If you're familiar with Astro, you are good to go.

Optimized out of the box

Lightweight and fast. Packed with Astro, tailwind and Sass goodness, so your pages load blazing fast—right from the start.
All the pages are configured in a way that are SEO-friendly and defined with markdown.

Looks great, day or night

With built-in dark theme support, your site will look amazing no matter what time of day your users visit. Or what their eyes prefer.

Forget convoluted theming systems. alfadev-astro-starter keeps it simple and extensible, so you can focus on your design and let the code work fori you.

Why alfadev-starter?

Because every content-driven page needs a lot boilerplate to initialize, and we provide you with the tools and guidelines needed to focus on what's important.

Simple

Avoid Complexity, small yet not useless. Loaded, but not overwhelming.

Up-to-date

Using the latest version and best practices of Astro, tailwindcss and Sass

SEO Friendly

Boost your site's visibility with built-in SEO optimizations, including automatic sitemap generation using astrojs/sitemap.

Extendable

Add or change content just by editing markdown files, so it stays in one place regardless of the code.

Focused

Put you effort on what's important, not on small details. Dont worry about that damn shadow.

Customizable

Every corner of the page can be easily changed to fit your needs.

Dark Mode Support

Fully supports dark theme with an easy-to-extend theming system.

Lightweight

Fast and efficient. Every asset is optimized to ensure minimal load times.

Modular

Built with reusable components that simplify adding or editing sections of the page.

Performance-First

Designed with best-in-class performance metrics to improve the user experience.

Content-Centric

Focused on delivering content seamlessly, making it ideal for portfolios, showcases, or landing pages.

Developer-Friendly

Crafted for developers who value simplicity, flexibility, and modern tools.
